## About Iron Ore
Iron usually exists in compound, especially in iron oxide. Several kinds of valuable iron ores are magnetite, hematite, limonite and siderite respectively. The grade of iron ore refers to the mass fraction of iron element in iron ore, say, the iron content. For example, if the grade of iron ore is 62, the mass fraction of iron element is 62%.
